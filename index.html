DimSumJS

What's all this then?

DimSumJS is a meetup designed to bring JavaScript developers together through dim sum and conversation.

That sounds a lot like SushiJS

Yes it does. We applaud SushiJS but meet in a different part of town and eat dim sum instead.

Why didn't you just suggest a second SushiJS somewhere else?

Because although sushi is delicious, sometimes you just want little steamed parcels of joy.

Where do you meet?

Currently there's a meetup in Chinatown, Central London. Others may follow.

How does it work?

Grab a ticket when a meetup is announced, then we'll book a table. Everyone turns up and pays for what they eat.

How much will I spend?

Typically you'd order three dishes and tea which would cost about £12.

Tips are usually included in the bill.
